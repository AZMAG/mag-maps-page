MAG Maps & Data Workshops provide online training in two of our most popular map viewers – the Arizona Demographics and the Arizona Employer viewers – as well as an introduction to our wide array of publicly available data visualization tools and resources.

Participants choose the segments they are most interested in. Stay for all three hours or choose any of three one-hour segments focusing on the following topics:

  • 9-10 a.m.    Population and Demographics
  • 10-11 a.m.    Economy and Employers
  • 11 a.m.–12 p.m.  Healthcare, Education, Recreation, and Transportation

All training will be held online via Zoom. One registration signs you up for all three segments. You can join the webinar at any time to get the information you’re looking for.

Select a date that fits your schedule. Click on a date above to register via Eventbrite. Registrants will receive an email confirmation with the Zoom meeting details. There is no charge for the workshops so feel free to share the link to this training page with your colleagues.

Come and explore MAG data resources, learn how to use the MAG map viewers to conduct searches for demographic and employer data, and discover other maps and data gems offered at MAG.

Training is free and open to the public, and is especially helpful to anyone involved in community assessment, economic development, grant writing, market research, and planning.

If you have any questions, please contact us maps@azmag.gov or (602) 254-6300.
